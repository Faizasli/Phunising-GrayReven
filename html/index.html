Punishing: Gray Raven (战双帕弥什) or simply PGR is a 3D post-apocalyptic and sci-fi action RPG developed and published by Kuro Games (库洛游戏). It was first released in China on December 5, 2019 and a year later on December 4, 2020 in Japan. The global release debuted on July 16, 2021.

Fast & Dynamic Gameplay


Experience intense hack-and-slash action with responsive controls and super smooth animations. Each character has a unique fighting style, supported by a strategic combination of attacks, dodges and skills based on the ping orb system. Take on waves of Corrupted enemies with epic combos and stylish moves!


‎

Deep Stories in the World of Cyberpunk


The “Punishing” virus has devastated the world, and humanity is on the brink of destruction. As the commander of the Gray Raven Squad, it's your job to reclaim Earth from the clutches of the Corrupted. Enjoy an emotionally charged storyline with deep narratives, mystery, and betrayal that will capture your heart.

‎

Compelling Characters

Each character in PGR comes with a unique design and strong personality. From agile swordsmen to deadly snipers, each unit has an interesting background and an important role in the storyline. Choose your character based on the appropriate fighting style, master their skills, and form the best team to face the challenges at hand.

Constructs: Humanity's Last Fighters

In a world devastated by the Punishing virus, humanity's last hope is in the hands of the Constructs-cyber-enhanced warriors created to reclaim Earth from darkness. Each has strengths, skills, and a story that makes them unique.
